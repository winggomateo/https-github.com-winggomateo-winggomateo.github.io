December 2021 â€“ Visual Identity, Illustration, Packaging Design & Print Design

Twelve Twelve is a company specializing in selling Cannabis sativa seeds for customers to grow at home. Twelve Twelve emphasizes a sustainable approach to marijuana cultivation, therefore its visual identity reflects the approach through an off-white and brown color palette. For printed matter, non-bleached organic paper is always used, inspiring the overall brand color palette.

The logo embodies the theme of cultivation as it resembles a blooming flower. Additionally, the logo has 12 petals on the interior, and 12 round bumps on the exterior of the icon, referring back to the company name and a ratio of light to dark (12:12) per day used in growing Cannabis sativa.

This project was part of a class assignment to design the identity for a fictional cannabis related company.

Twelve Twelve's Marketing asset's continues using a kraft-paper texture, connecting it with the rest of the brand. Although, there is also the introduction of hand-drawn illustrations of Cannabis sativa plants, emphasizing Twelve Twelve's association with cultivation.
