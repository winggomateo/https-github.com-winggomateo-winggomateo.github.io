Circulo Playing Cards

November 2020

I created Circulo Playing Cards for the purpose of cardistry. Cardistry is an art relating to the performance of card flourishes. Contrasting from magic, cardistry is meant to be focused on visual impressiveness in the difficulty to perform specific flourishes. The cards have a simple design with a pattern of repeating full and hollowed circles. While they are visually interesting, I did not choose to print the Circulo design because I felt it did not contain the vibrance I wanted for cards used for cardistry.
