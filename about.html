About

B. 2002, New York, NY – He/Him

Mateo is a graphic designer and photographer currently studying at New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized study. With design, Mateo has practiced with and created Visual Identity’s, printed matter, web graphics, and packaging designs. His photographic work, while focusing on portraiture, is wide-ranging, consisting of both film and digital photography.

Having a love for creative fields overall, Mateo is open to working on and learning anything – photography, design, illustration, and video production. Feel free to reach out anytime if opportunities are available.
