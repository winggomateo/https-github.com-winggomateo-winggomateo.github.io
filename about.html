Who is Mateo?

B. 2002, New York, NY. Based in Washington, D.C. – He/Him

Currently, I am a student at New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study. At NYU, I plan to concentrate on graphic design and other mediums of visual communication, and additionally explore the impact of design on contemporary world. I am looking for any opportunities to contribute to a project while further developing my abilities in the field.

I'm am open to almost anything as I love the creative field overall - photography, painting, illustration, and video production. For this reason, feel free to reach out any time if any opportunities are available.
